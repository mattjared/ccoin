We Pay You!

With this program, we sign a lease agreement with the property owner to rent space (the laundry room) in your building. In addition to paying you rent for the space, we pay for all installation, equipment, service, property tax, liability insurance, and replacement cost.

With this program, we provide the equipment as described above, except the property owner assumes full responsibility for collecting / counting the coins, the cost of any vandalism, and property / liability insurance. Additionally, you pay a flat fee for service and equipment, regardless of usage.

We sell and service Speed Queen and Whirlpool Commercial laundry equipment. The owner is responsible for all expenses after factory warranties.
