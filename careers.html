Careers

Looking for a career, tired of switching jobs and always starting over? We are a small successful family business with over 57 years of experience and most employees have been working at our company for 18-40 years.

We are currently seeking a skilled and experienced maintenance technician to perform basic electrical and mechanical repair of equipment. As a maintenance technician you will be repair and/or upgrade equipment, conducting tests on equipment, rebuilding machines, cleaning, painting, and installation.

Requirements

As a maintenance technician you must be able to conduct operational tests and repair equipment tests and repair equipment by disassembling required components and repairing and/or replacing faulty parts then reassembling and adjusting.

Additional requirements:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Vocational or Technical School and or/equivalent maintenance experience
  • Mechanical ability
  • Ability to work with hand and power tools
  • Ability to follow written and verbal instructions
  • Ability to life 75 pounds
  • Your own tools
  • A clean appearance
  • Good people/customer service skills
  • A good driving record
  • Ability to pass a drug test, we are a drug free work place

Benefits

We understand that our employees work hard, so we offer competitive compensation and an excellent benefits package. We value a happy, healthy, and supportive work environment that provides our employees with career opportunities for those seeking advancement and professional growth.

  • 401(K) Retirement
  • Profit Sharing Plan
  • Career coaching and mentorship
  • Medical Health Care Package
  • Paid Vacation and Holidays
  • Competitive hourly wages
  • On the job training
  • M-F 8am to 5pm
